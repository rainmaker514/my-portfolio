About Me

I am a dedicated Fullstack Web Developer based in Hollywood, FL. With a strong background in computer science and a passion for creating innovative solutions, I strive to deliver high-quality projects that meet client needs and drive positive outcomes.

As a freelance web developer, I have been actively involved in creating web applications and software solutions for clients. Leveraging my expertise in fullstack development and strong technical skills, I deliver customized solutions that align with client requirements and industry best practices.

I hold a Bachelor of Computer Science from Florida International University in Miami, FL. This comprehensive program provided me with a solid foundation in computer science principles and equipped me with the skills necessary to tackle complex software development challenges. Additionally, I earned an Associate of Arts degree from Broward College in Davie, FL, which served as a starting point for my understanding of various aspects of technology.

I am committed to continuous learning and staying updated with the latest technologies. This dedication allows me to deliver high-quality projects and exceed client expectations. I am always open to new opportunities and collaborations that allow me to contribute my skills and expertise to impactful projects.
